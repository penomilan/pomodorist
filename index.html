POMODORO TECHNIQUE

1500
The Pomodoro Technique is a simple time management technique that breaks your time down into chunks. You work for 25 minutes and then take a five-minute break, wash, rinse, repeat two more times, and finally work for another 25 minutes and take a 15-minute (or longer) break. This timer counting down for you from 25 minutes, and all you have to do is to click on "START". Enjoy!
